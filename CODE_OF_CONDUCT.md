# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

We pledge to make participation in RepoRook welcoming and harassment-free for everyone. Be respectful, assume good intent while evaluating technical claims rigorously, and focus criticism on work rather than people.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, publishing private information, or intentionally exposing another person's credentials or vulnerable code.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers. Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when necessary to protect the community. This policy is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1.
